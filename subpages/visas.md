Permanent residents of the countries of the Schengen area are exempt from a visa to visit Germany.
Other countries, such as United States of America or Canada, have bilateral agreements allowing their citizens to visit Germany without visas.
Other visitors may require a short-stay visa. For more information, visit the <a href="https://www.auswaertiges-amt.de/en/einreiseundaufenthalt/visabestimmungen-node">Federal Foreign Office of Germany</a> website or check with a local German consulate about visa requirements for your country of residence and/or nationality.
